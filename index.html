Hey, it looks like this is your first time running DooM Launcher!

You will need a couple of things to get started,

  • The appropriate IWAD from DOOM 1 (doom.wad) or DOOM 2 (doom2.wad).
  • A Doom Engine Source Port.
  • Some Custom WADs!
After you've obtained and set them up as you would like on your system, you should be able to use DooM Launcher now.

To get started, first click the "Select Engine" button - and navigate to the .exe of your Doom Source Port.

Now click on "Select WAD Folder", and navigate to your secret stash.

Now play some DooM by clicking the desired WAD from the list!
